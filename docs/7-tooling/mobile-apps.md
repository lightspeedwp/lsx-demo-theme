# Mobile App Recommendations for Interns

While most WordPress development happens on desktop, having the right mobile apps ensures you remain productive on the go. Install recommended iOS and Android apps from official stores and sign in using your LightSpeed Google account or service-specific login. Many mobile versions mirror desktop functionality and keep you connected to the team.

---

## 1. Essential Productivity Apps

-   Slack (iOS & Android) — Team chat, DM, channels. Install from App Store & Google Play. Sign in with LightSpeed email; configure notifications.
-   GitHub (iOS & Android) — PR/review, code & issues. Sign in with GitHub account.
-   Figma (iOS & Android) — View/comment on designs. Sign in with Figma account.
-   1Password (iOS & Android) — Password manager & autofill. Use biometrics for convenience.
-   Google Drive (iOS & Android) — Cloud files & docs. Sign in with LightSpeed account.
-   Loom (iOS & Android) — Record & access video messages. Sign in with Google/Slack.
-   Harvest (iOS & Android) — Track time/timesheets. Sign in and start timers from phone.
-   Fireflies.ai (iOS & Android) — Meeting transcripts & AI notes. Sign in/Google login supported.
-   Google Meet (iOS & Android) — Team calls & meetings. Enable camera/mic permissions.

---

## 2. AI Chatbot Apps

-   ChatGPT (iOS & Android) — GPT-powered chatbot for brainstorming and answers. Install from App Store & Google Play. Free version available; sign in with OpenAI account.
-   Claude (Anthropic) — Advanced AI assistant (availability varies by region). Install when available; use same handle as other profiles.
-   Gemini (Google AI) — Multimodal Gemini assistant (Pixel devices primarily). Use Gemini through Google App where available.

---

## 3. Browser & Testing Apps

-   Google Chrome (iOS & Android) — Main mobile browser for development/testing. Sign in to sync bookmarks. Extensions not supported on mobile.
-   Brave (iOS & Android) — Privacy browser with Leo AI. Enable Leo from sidebar.
-   Microsoft Edge (iOS & Android) — Cross-browser testing and Copilot integration.
-   Opera (iOS & Android) — Built-in VPN, alternate browser.

---

## 4. Tips for Mobile Productivity

-   Enable 2FA: Use your mobile device to secure all accounts.
-   Manage notifications: Configure critical alerts only (mentions, meetings).
-   Test on real devices: Use mobile browsers to inspect responsive design and performance.
-   Use Wi‑Fi: Download files and view videos over Wi‑Fi to save mobile data.
-   Refer to other guides:
    -   AI Tools Guide for mobile AI chatbot details
    -   Profiles Guide for usernames and sign-up
    -   Chrome Extensions Guide for browser extension info

Stay productive anywhere — these apps help you track tasks, join meetings, review code, record demos, manage passwords, and connect with the LightSpeed team while mobile.
